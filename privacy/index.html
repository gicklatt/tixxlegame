Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

In short: Tixxle collects nothing. There are no ads, no analytics, no accounts and no tracking. Everything the game saves stays on your device.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Tixxle ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in the mobile game Tixxle — Color Match Arcade (the "App"). By using the App you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App is designed to work without collecting personal data. We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number or any account details
  • Your location
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA / GAID) — the App shows no ads
  • Analytics, usage statistics or crash reports
  • Contacts, photos, microphone or camera data

The App does not require an account and does not ask you to sign in.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

Tixxle saves your game progress — high score, levels and stars completed, unlocked characters and daily-quest progress — locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or to anyone else. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes it.

4. No Advertising

Tixxle contains no advertisements and no ad SDKs. Nothing in the App is used for advertising or marketing purposes.

5. No Tracking

The App does not track you and does not link any data to your identity across apps or websites. On iOS, the App declares no tracking in its privacy manifest.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses no third-party analytics, advertising or social services. If you download Tixxle from the Apple App Store or Google Play, the store itself may collect data under its own privacy policy, over which we have no control.

7. Children's Privacy

Tixxle is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children. No personal data is needed to play.

8. Offline Play

Tixxle is fully playable offline. The App does not require an internet connection to function.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at gicklatt@gmail.com.
