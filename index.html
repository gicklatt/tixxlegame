TIXXLE

Tap to swap, chain combos

A fast color-match arcade game. Two characters hold their lanes, colored blocks race in — one tap swaps the lanes. Line up the color, score the match, keep your combo alive.

Inside Tixxle

Built for one thumb, tuned for reflexes

Combo chains that bend time

Every milestone in your combo slows the blocks down — reward for nerve, punishment for none.

6 collectible characters

Unlock six pixel characters as you clear worlds — each with its own look on the lanes.

Daily quests

A fresh set of goals every day — a reason to come back and a steady drip of rewards.

Atari-era craft

A hand-built retro palette — neon pixels on deep black — and a chiptune soundtrack synthesized note by note.

Two ways to play

Modes

Endless Run

Race the blocks

Chase a high score as the speed climbs. New colors arrive, lanes multiply — and power-ups (shield, slow-mo, magnet, burst) keep the run alive. One miss ends it.

100 Levels

Four worlds, one twist each

One hundred handcrafted stages across four worlds — color steal, a third color, full combo chains. Earn three stars on every stage.
